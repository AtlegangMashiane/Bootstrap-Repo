Intercommunication Circuit Design

Objective:
The objective was to design and build a simple intercommunication circuit using a microphone and earphones.

Materials:
  • Resistors: 1kΩ, 10kΩ, 120kΩ, 2x 3k3Ω, 100kΩ potentiometer
  • Capacitors: 1μF, 100μF
  • 9V Battery
  • Electret Microphone
  • PCB mount mono jack connector socket (2 pole, 3.5mm)
  • Soldering station, wires, function generator, digital oscilloscope, and multimeter

Procedure:
- The circuit was designed and simulated using Proteus software.
- Various resistors and capacitors were connected in parallel and series to establish the power flow.
- The microphone and headphone jack were connected to the operational amplifier IC 741.
- Voltage drops were measured, and the circuit’s integrity was verified using a multimeter.

Results:
Voltage measurements across different components confirmed the correct operation of the circuit with no faults. A practical intercommunication system was successfully created.

Conclusion:
The project achieved its objective of creating a functional intercommunication circuit, demonstrating proficiency in circuit design, simulation, and testing.
